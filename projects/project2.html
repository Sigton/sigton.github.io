Legend Rush

This is the best version of LEGEND RUSH. The point of the game is to collect all of the gemstones at the end of each level by jumping through the platforms. All levels are possible! More levels coming soon! My Best: 66 secs Controls: Left/right arrows to walk left/right. Up arrow to jump. Space to attack.
