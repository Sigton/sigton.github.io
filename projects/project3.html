3D Flying Simulator

Controls: W/S - Pitch (vertical) control A/D - Yaw (horizontal) control Left/right arrows - Roll control Up/down arrows - Thrust control R - Reset The 'T' stands for thrust. Space starts at altitude 10,000 Eventually this will no longer be a simulator but a full aerobatics game, where you will have to dodge obstacles and get to destinations as fast as you can!
